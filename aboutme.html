About Me

As someone that finds fascination in how things work, I started working on cars which is still a major hobby I have today! Around the age of 18, my father introduced me to building computers. I then became interested in the tech world and started by building my own gaming PCs. After years of tinkering with engines and PCs in my spare time, I found something I was truly amazed by. Coding. After playing around in some of the free teaching software programs, I decided to take a leap and enroll in Austin Coding Academy. Through these courses I am looking to gain knowledge and basics of the common languages used today. With that knowledge, I plan to continue to grow as a developer and push my boundaries in a career that continues to evolve and reach new heights.
